3D Tetris Gameplay Instructions

Game Mode:
m- resets to normal playing mode
b- sets to Chromadepth mode
p- pause

Game Control:
Left- Move block left
Right- Move block right
Up- Rotate block
Down- Move block down
Space- Drop block down

Camear Control:
a- Move Camera to the Left
d- Move Camera to the Right
w- Move Camera Forward
s- Move Camera Backwards
r- Move Camera Up
f- Move Camera Down
To Look around, click and drag the mouse anytwhere in the window
